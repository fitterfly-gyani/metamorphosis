FAQ

How can I enroll?

You need to nominate atleast 10 employees for the weight or diabetes management program. Once they are nominated you can either sponsor the program for the employee or they can pay for it themselves.

How can I get my employees to enroll?

You can follow the steps (hyperlink above answer). This 90 day program will help them achieve their health goals and live a healthier, happier, and more productive life.

How many employees can enroll?

There is no upper limit. You can nominate & enroll as many employees as you want to. The minimum number of employees to participate is 10.

How can I convince my employee?

The metamorphosis challenge will help them win their health goals and fun rewards in an immersive and engaging challenge. It will result in enhanced productivity, reduced insurance premiums, reduced stress, increased productivity and will be a unique team-bonding opportunity for them. For people with diabetes, it will help them reduce HbA1c levels, medicines, insuline dosage, and lose weight. And for those with stubborn weight issues it willl help them lose weight sustainably, scientifically, and safely.

How will you decide the rewards?

1. Finishing daily task: 50 points/member (maximum in a day 250)

2. Attending calls (individual/group) – 50 points for individuals per call. For group calls – 50 points per team member joining the call to a maximum of 250 points per call.

3. Completing weekly challenges – 5000 points per team.

4. Bonus for meeting program goals – 10,000 per outcome met per team member.

What are the rewards?

1 Overarching quarterly challenge: First winner will get Iphone 13, first runner up will get a Garmin watch and the third one will get Samsung tablet (TBD)

3 monthly challenges: Top three winners will get INR 5000 Amazon vouchers

12 weekly challenges: Top three winners will get INR 2000 Amazon vouchers

All the Rank 1’s will also get one 90-day subscription(Reset or Diabefly) for their friends and family from Fitterfly

Each challenge would have its own set of rewards. The participants would get points on each of these challenges and accordingly their score will change on the leaderboard

Minimum participation needs to be 60 days

What kind of weekly challenges will be part of this initiative?

The challenges will be divided into three categories - nutrition, exercise, and stress+sleep. All the challenges will have tracking parameters. Some exaples are - Surya namaskar challenge, protein in every meal challenge.

Who should enrol for this program?

People who have been diagnosed with metabolic health conditions such as - prediabetes, type 2 diabetes, PCOS, hypothyeoidism, high cholesterol, hypertension, high BMI or body fat. However, there are some criteria for joining the program, please refer to the same below:

HbA1c: 6.5 and above

BMI: 25 and above

Only Type 2 diabetes

Age: 25 and above

Not for pregnant women

How long is the challenge?

The Metamorphosis program/challenge is a 90 days long program. However, as part of the program, the participants will be given several weekly and monthly challenges. They will be notified about the soon, well in advance.

How will I track their progress?

You will be given access to a dashboard in the form of a microsite. Where you will be will be track to track the progress of your team members in real time.
