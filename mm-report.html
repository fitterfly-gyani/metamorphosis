Your Metabolic Health Score Card
is divided into the following:

Summary
  • Your Metabolic Health Score
  • Average Metabolic Health Scores of your organization and the rest of the population
  • Areas requiring immediate action, monitoring and maintenance
Diseases Risk
  • Details of your risk (based on international standards) of developing diabetes, metabolic syndrome, or cardiovascular disease over the next 5-10 years
Activity Score
  • Activity score
  • Effects of your activity score on overall metabolic health
  • Ways to improve your activity score
Sleep & Well-being Score
  • Analysis of your overall psychological well being and sleep quality
  • Their effects on your metabolic health
  • Ways to improve your sleep and well-being score
Healthy Eating Assessment
  • Categorizes eating patterns into a health benefit score
  • Action plan based on the score
Characteristics Score
  • Categorizes risk due to characteristics such as age, BMI, waist circumference, disease status, smoking, etc.
  • Suggestions of corrective measures to lower risks
Lab and Biochemical Assessment
  • Detailed scoring based on your scores and normal laboratory values
  • Explanation of associated diseases and complications
Improving your Metabolic Health Score
  • Simple tips to improve your metabolic health
  • Suggestions of digital therapeutic interventions for significant improvement
